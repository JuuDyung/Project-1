Korean Food - Top 5 I've chosen

1. Korean BBQ

The reason why I like Korean meat, which is different from steak or barbecue meat eaten abroad, is because of the style of eating meat.

Put the meat on the fire board on the table and then enjoy after roasting it.

2. Fried Chicken and Beer

We can have fried chicken in all around the world, but Korea has various kinds of fried chicken.

For example, seasoned spicy chicken, soy sauce chicken and so on. Korean people usually have fried chicken with beer.

That's really good taste and I want to recommend to have them.

3. Tteok-bokki

I like tteok-bokki very much. It is stir-fried rice cake and called 'tteok-bokki'.

It is a rice cake with red hot pepper paste. The rice cake is a finger size white chewy food made with white rice.

The red hot pepper paste is made with flour and red hot pepper or Jalepino pepper.

It is spicy, but we can choose the level of spicy before we order it.

You can add fish cake, boiled egg or even ramyun in it.

4. Bibimbap

Bibimbap is the most famous Korean food and is a world-famous health food. Bibimbap is kind of mixed rice.

The ingredient of it is a bowl of warm white rice topped with namul which is seasoned vegetables and gochujang

which means chili pepper paste. Additionally, the fried egg or sliced beef is on the very top of Bibimbap.

We can have it by mixing it all the ingredients together.

5. Samgyetang

Samgyetang is the representative invigorating food of Korea. Most of Korean people have it in summer,

especially on the hottest day. Samgyetang is a food that is eaten by boiling ingredients such as ginseng and jujube together with chicken.

It is good for rejuvenating your health. It's a great menu for foreigners to eat without burden.
